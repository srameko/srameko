# Hi, I'm Ondřej 👋

I'm a forensic analyst and incident response manager, currently working in a CERT team at a Fortune 100 company. I've been dealing with incidents ranging from misconfigurations to nation-state actors and ransomware — across on-prem, cloud, and hybrid environments.

My main focus is **log analysis**, **EDR forensics**, and **threat hunting**. I've also been a member of [Locked Shields](https://ccdcoe.org/exercises/locked-shields/) since 2014 — including the 2017 winning team.

When I'm not in front of a SIEM, I teach cybersecurity at [Czechitas](https://czechitas.cz), helping people who are new to the field find their footing.

### 🔍 What I work on

- 🧠 Threat hunting & Cyber Threat Intelligence
- 🔬 Incident response & digital forensics (SIEM, EDR)
- 📊 Log analysis — alert triage, correlation, timeline reconstruction
- 🎓 Security education & workshops
